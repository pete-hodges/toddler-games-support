Frequently Asked Questions

How do I exit a game?

Press and hold the home button (house icon) in the top-left corner for about half a second. This long-press design prevents toddlers from accidentally leaving the game.

How do I save a drawing in Finger Paint?

Tap the save button (camera icon) in the toolbar. The drawing will be saved to your device's photo library. You'll be asked to grant photo access the first time.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Toddler Games Fun works entirely offline. No data is ever sent or received.

Does the app collect any data?

No. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What ages is this app designed for?

The games are designed for children ages 1-4, but older kids may enjoy them too.
