Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Overview

Toddler Games Fun ("the App") is designed for children and their families. We take privacy seriously, especially for our youngest users. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected from users of any age
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information are requested
  • No photos, videos, or audio recordings are collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No device identifiers are tracked

Internet & Network Access

The App functions entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, communicate with servers, or transmit any data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads displayed in the App.

Photos

The App includes an optional drawing feature that allows saving artwork to the device's photo library. This requires the user to grant photo library access through a system permission prompt. No images are uploaded or shared externally. This is the only device permission the App requests.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for use by children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids category apps. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of unauthorized collection of children's information.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with an updated date.
